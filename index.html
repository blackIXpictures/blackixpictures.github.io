About

Made by
genre obsessives,
for genre obsessives.

Black IX Pictures is an independent production company based in Los Angeles, built around a singular obsession with genre filmmaking — thriller, horror, sci-fi, and the strange spaces between them.

We make films for audiences who appreciate atmosphere over jump scares, ideas over spectacle, and stories that linger long after the credits roll.
